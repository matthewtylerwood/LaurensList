What is Lauren's List?

Lauren's List is a web site for customers to find services that they need.
At the same time Lauren's List is also a web site for contractors to advertise their services to potential customers.
The benefit of using Lauren's List is that all the contractors are rated by previous customers.
Our customers can browse through varies contractors and decide if those contractors are right for them.
Lauren's List makes it easy to see the contractors with the highest ratings, and also makes it easy for customers to leave their own ratings and reviews
